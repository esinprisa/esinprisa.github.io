Methodology

«Español Sin Prisa» helps students who are not in a hurry to learn Spanish.

Our mission is to show a student an easy and fun way to learn Spanish without haste.

Our teachers are certified professionals

Our teachers are certified professionals with rich experience in teaching foreign students. Our expertise lets us teach students effectively and in an interesting manner.

Teacher and student communicate 50/50

We expect students to actively participate in conversation with a teacher to become fluent in language. Depending on the course level, students should speak 50% of class duration.

Learn at your schedule and travel to practice

After taking a course at the pace you want, spending 1-2 hours a week learning, you will be able to speak Spanish and travel with a great pleasure!

Levels

You can start as the absolute beginner or master your skills at advanced level.

We offer all language levels of Spanish and a special course to prepare for DELE exam. You will learn through speaking, reading, listening, and writing.

Advantages

Not only you learn a language, you learn Colombian culture.

The cultural approach of the class makes it even more interesting - not only you will learn words and grammar but you will discover a whole continent.

Colombia has a very neutral and popular Spanish accent, we also use grammar in a very general way - anywhere you are people will be able to understand you what is very useful for people who would like to travel through Latin America.
